Cognitive Biases (The Errors)

Understand the systematic errors in thinking that lead to irrational choices and flawed predictions.

Confirmation Bias

#Error

The tendency to seek out, interpret, and favor information that confirms or supports one's prior beliefs or values.

Sunk Cost Fallacy

#Error

The tendency to continue a venture or investment simply because one has already invested time, money, or effort into it.

The Planning Fallacy

#Error

Systematically underestimating the time and resources required to complete a task, even while knowing similar past tasks ran over.

Online Shopping Biases

#Application

A practical guide to how biases like scarcity and anchoring are used to manipulate purchasing decisions.

Frameworks (The Tools)

Actionable mental models and organizational systems that guide proactive, structured, and successful thinking.

Inversion

#Method

The technique of solving a problem by looking backward; defining and avoiding what you absolutely do not want.

Second-Order Thinking

#Method

Moving past the immediate, obvious consequence of a decision and anticipating the subsequent, less obvious effects.

Map is Not the Territory

#Principle

The recognition that the model (or map) of reality is not reality itself, preventing over-reliance on theories and abstractions.

Career Inversion

#Application

A framework using inversion to define career failure points first, ensuring you avoid burnout and strategically find success.

PARA-Folio System

#System

A universal method for organizing your digital life around action (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives), not just subjects.
